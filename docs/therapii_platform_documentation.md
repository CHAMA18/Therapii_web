## 1. Executive Summary

### What is Therapii?

**Therapii** is a revolutionary mental health platform that extends the therapeutic relationship beyond the confines of scheduled sessions. By leveraging advanced AI technology trained specifically by each patient's therapist, Therapii provides continuous emotional support while maintaining clinical boundaries.

### The Problem We Solve

Traditional therapy operates on a session-based model—typically one hour per week. This leaves patients without professional support during the remaining 167 hours, often when they need guidance most. Critical moments of anxiety, depressive episodes, or emotional challenges don't wait for scheduled appointments.

### Our Solution

Therapii creates a seamless bridge between sessions through:

- **AI Companion**: A personalized AI trained by the patient's own therapist
- **Voice Check-ins**: Verbal processing and emotional expression
- **Direct Messaging**: Secure patient-therapist communication
- **Clinical Insights**: AI-generated summaries for therapist review
- **Educational Content**: Curated therapeutic resources via the Journal Platform

## 8. AI Companion System

### 8.1 Architecture Overview

The AI Companion is powered by OpenAI's GPT-4o model, accessed securely through Firebase Cloud Functions.

```
┌─────────────────┐     ┌──────────────────┐     ┌─────────────────┐
│   Patient App   │ ──▶ │  Cloud Function  │ ──▶ │   OpenAI API    │
│                 │     │  (API Proxy)     │     │   (GPT-4o)      │
└─────────────────┘     └──────────────────┘     └─────────────────┘
         │                       │                        │
         │                       ▼                        │
         │              ┌──────────────────┐              │
         │              │    Firestore     │              │
         │              │ (Conversations,  │◀─────────────┘
         └─────────────▶│   Summaries)     │
                        └──────────────────┘
```

### 8.2 Training System

Therapists configure AI behavior through multiple layers:

**Layer 1: Practice Profile**
- Specializations and therapeutic models
- Communication style preferences
- Practice philosophy

**Layer 2: Custom Instructions**
- Free-text guidance for AI behavior
- Specific techniques to emphasize
- Topics to avoid or handle carefully

**Layer 3: Patient Context**
- Therapeutic goals from onboarding
- Session history (future enhancement)
- Crisis protocols

### 8.3 Safety & Guardrails

| Guardrail | Implementation |
|-----------|----------------|
| **No Medical Advice** | System prompt explicitly prohibits diagnosis |
| **Crisis Detection** | Keywords trigger emergency response protocol |
| **Boundary Setting** | AI clarifies it's not a replacement for therapy |
| **Human Escalation** | Encourages contacting therapist for serious concerns |
| **Content Filtering** | OpenAI's safety systems plus custom rules |

### 8.4 Conversation Summaries

AI-generated insights help therapists prepare for sessions:

**Summary Components:**
- Key themes discussed
- Emotional state indicators
- Concerns raised
- Coping strategies discussed
- Recommended follow-up topics

## 9. Security, Privacy & Compliance

### 9.1 Data Protection

| Measure | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Encryption at Rest** | All data encrypted in Firestore |
| **Encryption in Transit** | TLS 1.3 for all communications |
| **Authentication** | Firebase Authentication with email verification |
| **Authorization** | Firestore Security Rules enforce access control |
| **API Security** | OpenAI calls proxied through Cloud Functions |

### 9.2 HIPAA Alignment

While Therapii itself is not a covered entity, the platform is designed with HIPAA-aligned practices:

- Business Associate Agreement (BAA) available
- Audit logging for data access
- Role-based access control
- Data retention policies
- Secure data deletion procedures

### 9.3 Access Control Model

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                         Admin Access                             │
│  • All platform data                                             │
│  • Therapist approvals                                           │
│  • Content management                                            │
│  • System configuration                                          │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
         ▲
         │
┌────────┴────────┐         ┌─────────────────────────────────────┐
│ Therapist Access │         │          Patient Access              │
│ • Own patients   │◀───────▶│ • Own data only                     │
```

| Data Type | Storage | Retention | Access |

## 11. Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: Is Therapii a replacement for therapy?**
> No. Therapii is designed to supplement, not replace, professional therapeutic care. The AI companion provides support between sessions but always encourages users to discuss significant concerns with their therapist.

**Q: How does the AI know about my therapy?**
> Your therapist trains the AI with their therapeutic approach, specializations, and communication style. The AI also learns from your onboarding information and therapeutic goals.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
> Yes. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Access is strictly controlled based on your role, and we follow HIPAA-aligned practices for data handling.

### For Patients

**Q: What if I'm in crisis?**
> If you're experiencing a mental health emergency, please contact emergency services (911) or a crisis hotline. The AI companion includes guardrails to detect crisis situations and provide appropriate resources.

**Q: Can my therapist see my AI conversations?**
> Your therapist receives AI-generated summaries highlighting key themes and concerns, but they don't see raw conversation transcripts unless this feature is explicitly enabled.

**Q: How do I switch therapists?**
> Contact your current therapist to discuss, then obtain a new invitation code from your new therapist.

### For Therapists

**Q: How do I get approved?**
> Complete your profile setup, including specializations and credentials, then submit for review. Our admin team will verify your information and approve your account.

**Q: How do I train the AI?**
> Navigate to the Training page to configure your AI companion's name, tone, and custom instructions. The AI uses your specializations and therapeutic models automatically.

**Q: Can I see what patients discuss with the AI?**
> You receive AI-generated summaries that highlight themes, concerns, and recommended follow-up topics without revealing every detail.

# Therapist Workspace

## Main therapist hub

The primary therapist workspace is `lib/pages/my_patients_page.dart`.

Main functions:

- load active patients linked to the current therapist
- show pending invitations
- open patient chat
- open patient profile details
- view active AI conversations
- view AI conversation summaries
- review voice check-ins

## Therapist gating

Therapist access is constrained by approval-aware UX.

Relevant component:

- `lib/widgets/therapist_approval_gate.dart`

This allows the product to keep therapist setup visible while limiting full access until approval conditions are met.

## Key supporting screens

- `listen_page.dart`
- `new_patient_info_page.dart`

- therapist profile and practice setup pages

## Conversation model

Human conversations are stored under:

- `conversations/{conversationId}`
- `conversations/{conversationId}/messages/{messageId}`

The workspace uses `ChatService` to:

- stream therapist conversations
- stream per-patient unread counts
- open linked chat threads

## AI summaries and voice

Therapists can review:

- AI conversation summaries from `ai_conversation_summaries`
- voice check-ins from `voice_checkins`

Primary services:

- `AiConversationService`
- `VoiceCheckinService`

## Therapist profile setup

Therapist configuration pages shape downstream experiences, including what clients see and how AI training metadata is derived.

Important profile-related screens include:

- `therapist_details_page.dart`
- `therapist_training_page.dart`

- `therapist_therapeutic_models_page.dart`
- `therapist_practice_page.dart`

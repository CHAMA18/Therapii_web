# Journal Platform

The journal system is a major product area implemented inside the same application.

## Reader portal

The main reader experience lives in `lib/pages/journal_portal_page.dart`.

Capabilities include:

- role-aware return path to the main Therapii session
- home, journal, and favorites navigation
- persisted sidebar width and portal destination
- article browsing
- favorites display

## Favorites

Users can save articles into:

- `users/{uid}/favorite_journal_articles/{articleId}`

The portal and feed surfaces now support direct favorite toggling from the bookmark icon.

## Article detail

Article detail rendering lives in:

- `lib/pages/journal_article_page.dart`

## Journal administration

Journal admin tooling includes:

- `journal_admin_dashboard_page.dart`
- `journal_admin_studio_page.dart`
- `journal_admin_analytics_page.dart`
- `journal_admin_team_hub_page.dart`
- `journal_admin_patients_hub_page.dart`
- `journal_admin_settings_page.dart`

## Content studio

The content editor in `journal_admin_studio_page.dart` supports:

- content library with article selection
- draft and published filtering
- creating, editing, and deleting entries
- content library collapsed by default
- large primary body editing area
- article metadata and schedule-related controls

Articles are stored under:

- `admin_settings/journal_content/articles`

## Operational notes

- Journal admin data is Firestore-admin-only by rule
- Reader favorites are per-user and self-scoped
- The portal can route back into admin, therapist, or client workspace depending on the signed-in account
